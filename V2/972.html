Chapter 972

In order to stop the magic circle, I stowed away the evil crystals. Then the magic circle began to shine brighter than ever before. Why? No, it was just a booby wrap for when it was lifted?

『Fran! Let’s teleport!』

「Okay!」

We used teleport to escape the hole at once. Immediately after that, a huge explosion occurred at the bottom of the hole. Fran unleashed her flame magic just before the teleport to destroy the magic circle. A bright red flame swirled around the bottom, and countless sparks of fire rose to the edge of the hole. A bright red tempest swirled at the bottom, and countless sparks of fire rose to the edge of the hole. However, the evil energy was still thicker than before. It seemed that the explosion did not destroy it. A few seconds later, the black aura-like evil energy erupting from the hole suddenly disappeared. However, that didn’t mean the evil energy had disappeared. Something that emitted powerful evil energy appeared in the hole. It looked down from the edge.

「... Evil person?」

『Yeah, it looks like an evil person, just like a doll that twisted its evil energy into a humanoid. Its outline wavered and wavered, which seemed to be similar to a ghost. As it was called a berserker, it raged like a trap until death when it was summoned. This must be a trap used to kill anyone who interfered with the activation of the magic circle. Even so, I feel a strangely high killing intent or just a stubborn relentless feeling.Named the berserker, it seemed like a trap used to kill anyone who interfered with the magic circle’s activation. But still, it had a strangely high killing intent. I think it was just enough to lure in the antidemons, including the giants, and cause them to go on a rampage. I transformed my decorative string to create countless spears. Moreover, I attacked the Ibilberserker from all directions so that there was no gap between them. Moreover, Fran continued to unleash her thunder magic from above. It may not be an opponent that was effective against paralysis, but her movements were dulled by the series of magic blows. In the end, she couldn’t even try to avoid them, and her entire body was pierced by the spear I had created. However, as long as it was not a normal lifeform, that was not enough to cause a fatal wound. The feeling coming from the spear was as if it had pierced through either a bubble or a bran. It was only a little touch thanks to its magical power, but if it hadn’t been for that, it would have just slipped through. Well, this was also within my grasp. I was able to do it quite well, probably because I was getting used to the skills. As long as his body was made up of Evil Spirits, Evil Spirit Absorption was a fatal ability for him. The Evil Gods, who were trying to escape by slipping through the spear, stopped and shouted in agony.The evil energy cloaking her body quickly died off and disappeared in less than a minute.

After confirming the complete disappearance of evil energy from the magic circle, we decided to return to the main force. When we reported on the evil crystals and the magic circle, they looked concerned. This was probably because it was more likely that the Dragon King was involved in this anomaly. The other adventurers had also come back, but they still couldn’t find any survivors. Both Isario and Hilt decided to conclude that the residents had been annihilated by antidemons. The second unit decided to round out the investigation and head for the nearest town from here. It was to replenish our supplies and share information. Maybe some kind of army? They were quite slow if they were to do so. They seemed to be heading in the same direction as us, but the speed was as if the civilians were mixed in.
