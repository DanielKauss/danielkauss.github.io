Chapter 1098

Fran gets electrocuted to knock the servant out. Well, maybe we just can’t kill this guy who’s officially recognized as an enemy?

She’s not in a room where I think the Viscount is, but in the basement.

We ran through the mansion, knocking off some servants and soldiers. Then, at the back of the mansion, we had finally found a staircase to the basement. But when we went down, we found no one there.

Well, we’ll know about that too.

『I’m a Viscount』

It seems he was hiding here. Maybe he planned to spend the time by staying here?

『Fran, there are some documents in this room. I’d like to keep them as they are, so I’ll try to take them over as little damage as possible』

「Nn.」

Fran nodded and already defeated the knight in a blink of an eye. The strongest in the mansion, yet no enemy of Fran.

After that, I beat up the Viscount with my telekinesis, and Fran sank a sorceress and her son, who looked like an orc, with a chopped neck.

They had already destroyed five of their houses.

At her first house, she learned that important people should escape to the basement. So, Fran started by looking for the basement and stepping on it.

Well, I didn’t get any hits other than at the start. Even so, I’ve been able to find a lot of things, such as hidden bookkeeping and illegal slaves.

This must be the situation that Esmeralda was aiming for.

The sixth house was very obedient. Apparently, the other troops were also steadily conquering the mansion, and it seemed that more than ten families had already been clamped down on the entire Royal Capital. I guess the word has already spread. They thought it would be better to get caught obediently than to go against them.

「Please wait. I’ve got a message from Esmeralda-sama」

「I got in touch? When?」

That’s what Blue said, but she didn’t seem to wear any communication magic tools. Fran tilted her head in confusion.

Then Blue held out something in her hand. It was covered with a small lump of sand.

「What’s this?」

The reason they called themselves Esmeralda in their ranks was probably because of the sand-based communications network. They scattered sand over a wide area, allowing them to monitor and communicate with each other.

Rather, it might be better not to move to concentrate on controlling the sand.

When Blue handed him the sand, Esmeralda’s voice reached me through Fran. It seemed that it was not a telepathy, but a bone conduction method of talking with sand vibration.

It’s only a few hours since we entrusted Dimitris to her, you know? I wondered if she had the help of Satya, but that doesn’t seem to be the case.

He said that his presence was so low that he had never been suspected before. That fact was actually because he was connected to Raidos. He probably didn’t have any relationship with Marquis Ashtner and acted together so that he wouldn’t be suspected when the time came.

The mansion stood quietly at the edge of the nobility district, a scene of commotion in many places. Due to the shadow of the royal castle, this was indeed a dark corner.

「Here?」

「Very well. And then there’s the mansion of Viscount Noare...」

Blue looked confused too. It really felt like a poor and low-ranking nobleman’s mansion and didn’t even have a gatekeeper. The walls were collapsed and the yard was not very well-kept. It was hard to believe that they were hiding a spy.

Still, Fran’s face was filled with motivation. I could feel some kind of presence, albeit faintly. Aside from the maids, it seems that the baron knew about them.

Aah, since she’s a member of Raydoss’s mercenary brigade, she’s recognized as an enemy since she’s always been in the top gear. Fran then questioned him again, looking down at the baron who was clutching his blood flowing in the thigh and moaning.
