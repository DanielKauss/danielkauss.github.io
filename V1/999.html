Chapter 999

After recovering from the neutralizing Velmeria, Fran and Urushi attacked Tilanaria.

Even though he was enhanced, his Divine Dragon Form was not activated. If we were now in his current state, it would have been an instant kill.

Like Velmeria, he managed to inflict some damage and then tightly blocked his movements with land magic.

「Urushi, keep an eye on them!」

「Woof!」

Now all that remains is the Dragon King Georg.

But――

「Grooaah!」

「Haaa!」

The battle between Georg and Isario was so fierce.

He was a world-class adventurer who used teleportation to move at high speeds, scattered flames and continued to defend himself.

If we got closer, we would either be torn apart by the aftermath of Georg’s god-speed, or our bodies would be turned into charcoal by Ignis’ fire heat.

There was such a hell there that we even had to be prepared to die.

『Georg’s strength is... too extraordinary』

Compared to Velmeria, who was in the same Divine Dragon Form state, this one was on a different level. Maybe its original performance was on a different level?

No matter how hard we intervened, we couldn’t even find that chance.

But over time, I wouldn’t be able to maintain my Divine Dragon Form. My durability value was starting to decrease.

Since I was not a dragonkin, I was not able to use my full power. Because of this, the backlash was weaker than usual. I wouldn’t be able to maintain that power for long, even if that was the case.

Thanks to the Divine Dragon Form, the manipulation of the Divine Attribute has been greatly improved. Now, we can unleash a blow with the Divine Attribute more than ever before...

I think he’s a dog-type because of those ears?

Fran was building up her strength without moving at all. She was just quietly bracing herself, looking for an opening to break into.

『Robe bastard is...』

She still didn’t move. Even after Velmeria and Tilanaria were captured, she showed no signs of slowing down or trying to save them.

We considered going for an attack, but it would be pretty bad if we got a bushes snake. We’ll have to take down Georg while he ignored us.

『Fran, how is it?』

I think he’s a dog-type because of those ears?

Apparently, she was trying to figure out Georg’s movements. And she was trying to get a feel for it.

One blow would be enough to break our balance. Isario wouldn’t miss that chance.

However, Fran wanted to perfect one of the few opportunities she had.

(Master)

『Can you go?』

(Not yet. Otherwise, I can tell Isario about Master?)

『Hmm? You mean you’re exposing my identity?』

(Nn. Master, tell Isario about your strategy by telepathy)

『Oh, I see.』

Fran wanted to concentrate even more on this. She seemed to be planning to divert even the slightest word to attack.

(Isario wouldn’t mind teaching me about Master)

『...Yeah. If it were Isario, I might be okay to reveal it to him』

(Nn.)

A momentary delay in timing may cause a fatal outcome. We should still try to time our timing with telepathy here.

I could hide my true identity, but it was better to reveal my true identity and gain trust here. Fran seemed to think so. Well, I guess she also wanted to keep her secret from Isario, whom she liked.

However, I couldn’t just surprise him by suddenly calling out to him. I think Isario would be fine, but I guess, in the unlikely event that I should.

Fran stepped forward a little without concealing her presence. That was enough to catch both Isario and Georg at the edge of their consciousness.

Then I raised my voice even further.

「Isario! You may be surprised now, but don’t be surprised!」

Maybe they could say something more? However, I could see Isario nodded lightly in response. It seemed that they knew that Fran was trying to intervene in some way.

After a short pause, I called out to him quietly.

『Isario. Can you hear me?』

(Hmm? A man?)

『That’s right. I’m Master. Well, don’t worry about the names since I’ll give you a thumbs-up in time. Oh, answer me is good. I’ll talk one-sidedly, so focus on the fight』

Isario seemed to be slightly flustered, but it didn’t seem to have had a significant effect on the battle. Okay, I wonder if I could do something about it.

『I’m on your side. But it’s hard to believe, so I’ll reveal my identity. I’m an intelligence weapon, the sword Fran was equipped with』

(Huh? Oops!)

『I know it’s hard to believe, but I want you to believe it now』

Isario glanced at Fran. However, Fran had already started to concentrate and could not afford to return the reaction to his gesture.

I had no choice but to transform my decorative string into the shape of a hand and returned a thumbs-up. So, she must have understood.

(Seriously?)

『Seriously. Then, Fran will go for one shot. Please sync it up』

(...I understand)

Alright, all that remains now is for Fran to land a blow on Georg.

Fran was still focused. Her concentration was so deep and heavy that she could not even feel the presence of her surroundings.

She even closed her eyes and completely went inside of herself. Shouldn’t we just look at Georg now?

But if I speak up now, it might interfere with Fran’s concentration. The only thing I could do was to trust Fran, wait, and protect her.
