Sophie and Mea greeted us at Nocta. We left earlier. Thank goodness, Sophie was surprisingly fine.

「Those who can’t fight, go out...? That’s not what I’m forced to do.」

Sophie expressed her anger, while Mea was bewildered. Since Sophie herself was on the side of the horde, she knew just how hard it would be for her to escape from Gordicia.

Mea seemed to be thinking about what would happen after she escaped from the continent of Gordisia. Even though she was such a young woman, she still knew how difficult it would be for her to accept refugees.

Being a frontier is quite hard work in this world. Well, it wasn’t an easy job on Earth, but the level of danger in this world is different.

In many areas that were still untouched by humans, it was a dangerous area in the first place where many magic beasts were inhabited.

Crowds of people, from both inside and outside the tower, started gathering around the edge of the pit.
