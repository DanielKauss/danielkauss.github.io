Chapter 1037

The remains of the castle of Trismegistus were still piled up. However, a part of it had already begun to be repaired.

I could see the outer wall of the castle gradually regenerating.

This method of regeneration was rather unique. Fran was staring at it with interest.

It wasn’t rising little by little like my regeneration skills, nor was the rubble returning to its original state like it could be regenerated in reverse.

First, a translucent wall was projected into the air, as if it were a holog map. Then, we could imagine that the hologma emitted a strong light, and in the next moment, the wall had acquired its substance.

Phobos emerged from the regenerated wall again, and the walls piled up one after another to resurrect. Even for me, this was a very strange sight.

「Hmm? Something’s glowing over there」

「He’s around where the throne is?」

I could see the light rising from the center of the pile of rubble. Like Isario said, it was probably the place where the throne room used to be.

We decided to head there for now.

As we climbed over the pile of rubble, I occasionally moved the rubble out of it and piled it up to serve as a foothold for our destination.

After about ten minutes of climbing the rubble, the scenery in front of us changed completely.

In the middle of the pile of rubble, there was a large space. It looked like a bowl, and looked like a crater.

An unharmed throne sat in the middle of it. Naturally, there was also the figure of the Lord who should be sitting on it.

As we looked down from the rubble of the mountain, we immediately looked away without interest.

There were no ceilings or walls, only the throne and the ground on which it was placed. The only thing that surrounded us was the remains of the castle.

It was so pitiful that I matched Trismegistus perfectly. I felt a strange feeling of unity, as if I had embodied the essence of this dragonkin.

A tall king sits in the middle of the remnants of the castle.

「...I’m sorry」

「I’ll be careful not to do anything like that」

Both Isario and Jayne must have felt close to me, looking down at Trismegistus with a complicated look on their faces.

We passed down a pile of rubble and approached Trismegistus.

「Isario, Fran, Jayne. What do you want?」

「Well, you’ve gotta check things out after all」

「Alright. I’ll answer your question.」

To put it bluntly, she was as inhuman as ever.

But I also felt a terrifying sense of emptiness in it.

This was the same atmosphere as before.

Her voice and expression looked like a machine, without the warmth of a human being. In fact, I had never seen this man shaken, and I thought that his heart had worn out during his long life.

But now that we’ve seen Trismegistus, we can tell that he used to be better than Jean. What we were watching was a dragonkin whose spirit had worn off and became like a machine.

But what we were right in front of us was a real doll. It looked like a fresh golem made from the flesh of a living being. It was as if we were right in front of it.

A puppet made from dragonkin’s flesh. There was no heart in it, only a mechanical response. That was what it seemed to me, but I understood.

Was it possible for a person to sense such emptiness?

Fran was not the only one. Everyone who came here felt an indescribable sense of uneasiness and fear that stopped them. Even Isario was holding his breath.

『That sword...』

(Juanaverta?)

『No. They look alike, but that’s all.』

At Trismegistus’ side, there was a silver Falcion. But it was different. I didn’t feel any presence, no power, no strange human presence. The sword had no heart.

Trismegistus must have noticed Fran’s strong gaze too.

「This is the perfect form of an Intelligence Weapon. Never utter unnecessary words or take unnecessary actions. It is a true Intelligence Weapon, perfect for rationality and convenience」

「You still think it’s okay to lose Juanaverta?」

Fran was concerned about something else.

(...I’m not.)

『Fran?』

(That’s not the perfect form of an intelligence weapon! You shouldn’t!)

Fran turned her head down and repeated the words of denial in her mind. Well, if I affirm Trismegistus’ words, I’m not that perfect. I wonder if I could accept it.

(Master is great not because of his strength. It’s great because he can talk. It’s fun because he can talk. It’s reassuring that he can talk. That’s not something amazing)

『Fran...』

It was a veto reaction that even made me hate him.

Maybe she had imagined what it would look like for me to become a sword. And she must have had a negative emotion that she couldn’t control over the sword, which reminded me of that image.

However, he didn’t want to mention it. He probably knew that telling Trismegistus was useless. Instead, he asked something else.

「I heard that Juanaverta hadn’t gone crazy for a long time. But why?」

「......I had a skill to not go crazy.」

Unusually for this man, he had a moment. His golden eyes turned to Falcion of Silver, and he narrowed slightly.

However, Trismegistus immediately replied with the same attitude as before.

「Skill?」

「Indeed. An Extra Skill called 『Permanent Loyalty』. It is a skill that determines a user and swears to follow them for good. In a sense, you can even say that it enslaves itself. In exchange, as long as the user exists, it can be a permanent invincible existence」

Does that mean that she can be immortal as long as the Lord is alive? And it doesn’t matter how old her mind is?

「You had that skill, but you still lost the source of your jealousy?」

「No. Instead of getting the original sin of jealousy, he was deprived of his eternal loyalty」

Aah, I see! That’s why I easily lost to the source of my jealousy and ended up like this.

「......Who took that skill?」

Fran pondered a little and asked. She didn’t want me to be like Fran’s slave with that skill, but she didn’t want me to be a sword. She just wanted to get a clue.

「I don’t know his name, but he was a Divine Swordsman」
