Sophie and Urushi appeared alongside with many people. Her――No, their song covered the entire battlefield. An adventurer’s song was sung by thousands of people. Space? The air? Anyway, the entire battlefield slowly healed Fran and Mea’s wounds with a faint golden light as if it was responding to the song. Ignoring even the effects of the divine attribute, I, Fran, and Mea were beginning to recover. They were probably due to this magical power that had the divine attribute. I guess it was because of this magical power that the divine attribute was possessed. The antidemons, on the other hand, were pausing their movements in confusion. With their entire bodies enveloped in powerful magical power, they don’t seem to know what to do. It’s probably the same as when an artificial intelligence bugged in an unexpected situation and stopped moving. That was happening on the entire battlefield. The effect was terrifying.

Many people were standing behind Sophie and Sophie as they smiled. They were wearing ordinary clothes. Some of them were wearing armor on top of it, but most of them were just wearing pots. However, they were standing in line behind Sophie with brave expressions on their faces. I wondered why I was so reliable just by looking at them. There were so many people willing to stand on the battlefield with us. That was the fact that was important.

『Nice job, Urushi! Urushi! It’s a reward for us later!』

「Woof woof!」

But it wasn’t just them that caught my eyes. Fran looked around Sophie and tilted her head.

Countless instruments were floating around Sophie as she smiled as she said those words. A keyed instrument, a string instrument, and a percussion instrument. Some of them looked strange. Ability to control instruments? However, all instruments were emitting tremendous magical power. Combined with all of them, they would be far beyond mine. Not an extraordinary magical tool. Could it be the Earth Sword Gaia that emitted the closest magical power I’ve ever seen? However, I didn’t feel an intimidating atmosphere here. Wait, I guess?
