I picked up Fran and Sophie, who were unable to move, with my telekinesis and moved as far away from them as possible.

We need to reduce Isario’s burden as much as possible. The other group members have also started to move. It would be quick if Urushi helped, but they couldn’t come out of the shadows yet.

I guess she must have put all her magic power into that one blow. Thanks to my cannibalism, my magic power had recovered a lot, so I could still move, but I might have been in danger if it hadn’t been for that.

As I was desperately away from them, I could feel the Divine Power moving behind us.

It was already more than 100 meters away, but I could feel the tremendous heat that Ignis was emitting.

The heat coming from Ignis created a kind of divine flame, which made the surroundings look distorted. Moreover, Isario’s body was burned, and smoke was billowing around him.

The flames swirling around Ignis must be so dense that even Isario could not control them.

As Isario swung Ignis, the flames danced around us. It was a beautiful sight, but the flames that touched the ground melted the earth and made a buzz. It was like a hellish pot.

Isario was clad in divine flames, but she must have been agitated by that sense of urgency. The giant antidemons wriggled wildly. What was unleashing were intense evil spirits.

Rather than an antidemon, it had an evil spirit so dense that it looked like a piece of the Evil God. Then, its body was transformed into a burst.

Dozens of tentacles were created and stretched toward Isario. I couldn’t help but wonder if they still had the power to make such a movement.

Against such an evil-looking antidemon, Isario calmly activated his technique.

「 Divine Sword Art, ‘Flaming Jail’」

What Ignis swung down was a small ball of fire. It looked like an unsuccessful Fire Ball.

But that didn’t seem to be the case. Right after the red ball flew in a straight line and collided with the giant antidemon――.

BOOOOOOOOOM!

A pillar of thick flames stood out from the place where the giant antidemons stood. The bright red flames rose in a straight line, as if it was about to pierce the sky.

Just as the tentacles of the giant antidemons wriggled wildly within the pillar of flame, it quickly burned out and disappeared.

Thanks to the control of Isario, we were only in a place where we could be at a temperature of about a Sacramento. However, the amount of heat trapped inside the pillar of fire would have exceeded our imagination. As the name of the technique implied, it was a hell of fire.

The excess energy leaking out into the surrounding area was enough to create a storm-like wind.

Just after this lasted for a few seconds, a sudden roar and shockwave disappeared. And at the same time, the pillar of flame also disappeared.

And the giant antidemons had completely disappeared. They had been completely exterminated by Isario’s Flame Jail. Even if I tried to detect their magic power, there was no sign of them.

Furthermore, the purple dome covering this area disappeared.

It was our victory.
