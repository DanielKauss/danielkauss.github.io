However, we could not leave the ship with someone we didn’t even know if we could trust. And it would be quite difficult to move north of the south even if we were to move by land.

That makes it convenient for a country that is trustworthy and close to Belioth.

「It’s the Kingdom of Kranzel」

「That’s probably the only way. The small countries in the south don’t know who is connected to Raidos Kingdom」

And so, we hurriedly decided to head for Barbra, a port city in the Kranzel Kingdom.
