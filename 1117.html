Chapter 1117

On the night after tomorrow’s transportation of supplies, Fran had visited Klimut the guild.

Fran knocked hard on the door, and there was a scream from inside. As we walked inside, Klimut looked bitter.

「I have told you not to go in before answering, didn’t I? It’s still the same as before! And I also ask that you be a little calmer when you say no.」

「I heard a strange voice, so I thought I had replied.」

「I-Impossible...... Now that my status is back to normal, I’m hearing better, but I’m not getting used to it yet」

So, even if he felt a presence and knew a knock, he would still be surprised by the loud noise. There were about twenty gem-like stones lined up on his desk.

He had a cloth in his hand, and seemed to be refining it. It was a magic stone. Moreover, it seemed to have quite strong magic power. As if to hide it from Fran’s eyes, Klimut put the magic stone in his bag.

「W-What is it?」

「Is that, Rhyn?」

「Y-yes. That’s right.」

Aah―, I’ve been through this kind of thing before. Back then, I had 10 threat levels C and D in total... But of course, that was not all.

So we really didn’t give him everything, huh? Back then, Fran negotiated to take five of them, but I guess Klimut expected all of them.

Maybe, even if all of them had been taken, if Fran had accepted the request, it would have been no problem for Klimut, wouldn’t it? So, Klimut was a true negotiator.

Klimt coughed lightly and changed the subject.

「You need something?」

「Nn. I want to ask you how to use Spirit Magic」

「Now that I think about it, you must have learned Spirit Magic」

「Nn.」

Fran nodded, and Klimut sighed wearily.

「Sigh...... That’s just as good as ever.」

She deposited her body deeply on the back and shook her head several times.

「It’s not something that can be easily learned by other races besides Elves...... I don’t mind if you don’t want to answer, but when and how did you learn it?」

「I’ve only recently learned that」

I beg for his teachings. It is indeed good for bugs to remain completely silent. I must tell him about them to some extent.

Fran was hiding about me too――.

I think he’s a dog-type because of those ears?

While she was explaining the situation, she suddenly fell silent.

「What’s wrong?」

Klimt looked puzzled.

『Fran?』

(Master, can I tell Klimut?)

Oh, so that was what she meant. From the moment we first met, there was a subtle atmosphere between Fran and Klimut. She didn’t have complete trust in the guild, and Klimut didn’t have complete trust in Fran.

The atmosphere here was one of respect for each other, yet one of discernment.

But before she knew it, Fran had completely lost her guard against Klimut. I already trusted Klimut. There was no denying it.

Besides, even if I kept silent, the Great Spirit would still see through me.

「Klimt, I have something important to discuss」

「Eh? Hah, hah. That’s pretty sudden.」

「Nn.」

Klimt looked as surprised as ever, but after Fran’s creation of a wind barrier, he realized that it was really important.

She straightened up her posture and stared at Fran.

「Let’s ask them.」

「About this sword」

Fran took me with her scabbard and held me aloft. Klimut’s gaze followed me, but a puzzled look came over me. Even though she knew that it was a vicious magic sword that emitted evil energy, she didn’t understand why she was going to bring up that subject now.

「Master」

『Yeah.』

「Hmm? Your voice now is......?」

『The sword in front of me. My name is Master, and I’m Master, an intelligence weapon. Nice to meet you!』

I purposely greeted her in a light atmosphere so as not to get too heavy, but...

「Eh? No, Eeh? The sword is talking... Eeh?」

Klimt was really surprised. Well, it couldn’t be helped. What a surprise, isn’t it more than a divine sword in terms of rarity? But maybe he was a little too surprised.

「I-I never thought I’d run into a legendary being in a place like this... T-This is bad for your heart!」

Klimt was holding his heart and was breathing heavily. Despite his handsome and super strong high-ranking adventurer, he was quite a coward, or rather, a dwarf. He seemed to have a lot of heart.

「I’ve heard that it exists in Gordicia, but this is my first time seeing it in person」

「You believe me?」

「There is no reason for you to lie. In the first place, I usually have conversations with spirits, so I can understand that telepathy is being released from the sword」

She was used to telepathy, and understood perfectly what I was saying.

「Have you ever been to Gordicia?」

「It was a place like a demonkin for spirit magicians. Just by summoning it, the spirits weaken, and it doesn’t heal. I think the reason why the elves were destroyed was because they can’t use spirit magic anymore, right?」

「I see. So, Master is your first Intelligence Weapon?」

「That makes sense. But, Master, isn’t it? It must be a strange name for a magic sword」

『I didn’t have a name for it, but Fran gave it to me! What a nice name, right?』

It was the usual exchange after we revealed our identity, but Klimut was indeed an adult.

「That’s a good name. I can feel Fran’s sense of it」

『You said that right? That’s a good name!』

「Hehe.」

Klimut gave Fran a gentle gaze, clearly smug. I knew he was a good person.

「But now I understand. So, Fran’s imbalance was caused by Master-san’s abilities?」

『Yeah. My ability is――』

When I briefly explained my power, Klimut grunted as he clutched his eyebrows. Well, I know how he felt. I’m sorry, sorry?

「A place like this where top-ranking magic swords are... on the level of a near-Divine Sword, right? Did I know it was better to reveal it to you?」

「Nn. I can trust you, Klimut」

「......Sigh. I can’t believe you’re saying that so straight. I’m not such a good person.」

She covered her eyes and looked up to the sky. Her murmurs didn’t stop. But Klimut, as expected, quickly remembered that she was in the middle of talking.

「So, what does Spirit Magic have to do with Master-san?」

『Actually――』

Klimut fidgeted his eyebrows and held back his stomach with his free left hand. He seemed to be hit by a double punch of headache and stomach pain.

「It’s so irregular that I can’t say anything about it. However, I can feel the presence of spirits inside Fran-san. It seems to have been weakened to such an extent that I couldn’t tell unless you said it」

I know that you supported Fran in the Great Spirit Battle. She should have gotten stronger for a moment, but after that, her presence disappeared again.

「Mar, are you okay?」

「We don’t know. Only in the legends do we know about the existence of a spirit from a human being. However, if Fran’s level of spirit magic increases, we might be able to supply her with power」

「Really?」

「Yes. But it’s not always easy to raise your level, you know? At first, the only thing I could do was to focus my attention on sensing the spirits」

「Even so, I’ll do my best.」

「Yeah, good luck」

Klimut shrugged his shoulders in disbelief. However, there was a soft smile on his face, like that of an old man who watched over his grandchildren.
