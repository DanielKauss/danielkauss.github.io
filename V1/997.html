Chapter 997

The place through the passageway was a strange place.

The dome-shaped room was not only spacious but also high, with countless gray roots stretched out all over it, some wriggling as if to beat.

The room of the Rasbos in a shooting game... I think something like this could happen.

A huge gray heart sat in the middle of it.

「Young miss, that must be the core of the Abyss Eater」

「Nn...... I can’t destroy that thing.」

Fran, whose spirit would normally have to try it, said that she couldn’t do it from the start. That was how extraordinary the core of the Abyss Eater was.

The evil energy mixed with the magic power emitted, the amount of power that lies within it, and its presence that made it hard to tell.

The aura he exuded was befitting of the Abyss Eater’s nucleus, created from pieces of the Evil God, and could easily be poisoned just by looking at it.

Even the Great Magic Beast in the lake that Weena Rhyn had destroyed seemed to be of a lower rank in front of this heart.

The shape of the heart was no different from that of a human heart. But it was big. It was probably more than 50 meters long.

Compared to the dragonkin standing in front of it, this one was even bigger.

「Velmeria! Tilanaria-sama! Are you okay!?」

「Oi oi, no matter how you look at it, they’re all insane」

Velmeria was among the dragonkin standing in front of their hearts. However, they did not respond to Frederick’s words and did not look sane, as Isario had muttered.

Velmeria and the red-haired dragonkin standing next to her were no longer in focus. That woman was Tilanaria, Velmeria’s mother and Divine Dragon priestess.

No, it’s not just them. The other large dragonkin man didn’t seem to be conscious either.

He was a terrifyingly magical dragonkin with golden scales like Trismegistus. Was this guy the Dragon King Georg? If so, why did he seem to be losing his mind?

「What are you trying to do?」

「Velmeria! Tilanaria! Shit! Georg, this is what you did!」

Frederick let out a yelp, but the dragonkin didn’t respond. Something was definitely wrong with them.

There was a small figure beside the three dragonkin. They wore deep robes, so they couldn’t even see their faces, but they looked like they were not dragonkin.

Is that robe bastard doing something? It seems to have an effect similar to Seliadot’s wards, but my appraisal doesn’t work on it inside the robe. Who is it?

Upon closer inspection, I saw that the robe bastard had his sword thrust at his heart. It was a magic sword with a rank of being capable of repelling appraisal. It might even have the power of a failed divine sword.

It didn’t look like it was attacking a nuclear weapon, but what on earth was it?

The shape of the sword was strange. At first glance, it looked to be two. It had two hilts, it looked as if the swords were crossed into a cross-section. But the overlapping parts were actually combined to form a single sword.

How do you use it? Like a boomerang?

I tried to appraise her again. I consciously kneaded the magic power of the Divine Attribute and activated the Heavenly Eye skill. By using the Divine Attribute, I can further enhance the skill.

Rather than simply raising the power output, my skill level should go a step deeper.

It makes it easier to manipulate when using body-enhancing techniques, and it makes it easier to reproduce one’s own image with magic. Since it was using Heavenly Eye, it might even work against a superior opponent.

『Gnrgh...... I see it!』

For just a moment. I wasn’t able to see everything, but I still got a lot of information.

『What the hell is that sword’s status...?』

Name: Falldown, Evil Erosion Sword――

ATK: 170

Mana Conductivity: A+

[Skill]

Automatic Repair, Evil Absorption, Evil Domination, Evil Erosion――

That’s quite a feat, but I’m too concerned about the rest. First, the name. Apparently, it’s a fusion of two swords, so I’m sure it’s better.

Besides, I’m also interested in his skills. It’s like he had the ability to manipulate evil energy. I’d like to see it up close, but we can’t go careless.

The dragonkin on the other side were all ready to fight at once.

『Fran! It’s coming!』

「Nn!」

「I’ll be the Dragon King! No one else will be able to do that!」

Trismegistus remembered Georg’s name. In other words, it meant that the man who was not interested in others remembered his name. That man was strong enough to remember his name.

Trismegistus showed no sign of movement. I guess it meant we could kill each other on our own. They wouldn’t be willing to help us, but wouldn’t they worry about Tilanaria or Velmeria being killed by them?

「My name is Tilanaria! Black Lightning Princess to ask for Velmeria!」

「Nn.」

From the looks of it, Tilanaria didn’t seem to be very strong. Velmeria, on the other hand, had activated Divine Dragon Form. Indeed, Frederick might have had a heavy burden on her.

『We’re in a lot of trouble too!』

(But we have no choice.)

The moment Fran readied me with determination, Velmeria and Frederick moved at once.

『We have to keep Velmeria and Frederick quiet in front of the robe bastard!』

「Nn!」

Fran also stepped forward and swung me down from above.

「Wrrrooof!」

「Haaa!」

The claws of Velmeria and I collided, creating a high-pitched sound. Not a sword, but a claw! Just like me...!
