Fran and Urushi, who had begun action to rescue their acquaintances, rushed to their side at once using teleportation.

The Raydoss soldiers were in complete chaos when the girl and the wolf suddenly appeared and began to attack the surroundings. While Fran and Urushi’s attacks blew the nearby soldiers away, I activated several of my land spells.

『Great Wall!』

We had surrounded all sides of the captives with walls and separated them from the surrounding Raydoss soldiers. Inside the wall, there was enough room to rest even if Urushi became huge.

Because of this, the soldiers of Raidos were still inside, but we quickly eliminated them. We only had to paralyze one of them, since there seemed to be someone important.

Outside the wall, the Raydoss soldiers were screaming and screaming. At this rate, it will take some time for them to break through the walls.

Fortunately, the handcuffs on the adventurers’ limbs were not magic tools. Besides, the slave collars had not yet been used.

As Fran cut off the shackles on their limbs, she began to remove the gagged legs on her own.

When Fran told them so, the adventurers let out a gasp of relief. Jugeon hugged his fellow adventurers, while Maia hugged Lydia and shouted with cheers. The others seemed to be relieved as well.

Judith thanked Fran with tears build up in the corners of her eyes.
