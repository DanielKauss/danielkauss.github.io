However, it was not like the equipment of the dragonkin in front of them was worn by skilled warriors. If they were an adventurer, they would probably be equipped by someone of Rank D or E or higher. The weapons they held were not strong enough to be of any rank. Some of them were as fast as lower echelons, and some didn’t even look like elites. That made them a force to be reckoned with. However, no matter what kind of opponent they were facing, they were not enemies with Isario. Even if he was still exhausted, he could not imagine that Isario would lose. Apparently, it was a forbidden medicine passed down to only a small part of the dragonkin. Only priestesses knew how to make it, and even their ownership was strictly limited. They give the dragonkin their permanent strength if they take it, rather than temporary. That’s a tremendous potion for the dragonkin. They can get stronger even though they haven’t leveled up. However, the side effects were also tremendous. First of all, they had a lot of refusal to allow their body to be transformed, and half of them died right after they took it. 「That way, you can greatly reduce their life expectancy.」

The Sacrament of the Dragon God is a liquid about the size of a squire. The strengthening can be activated by swallowing it down. So, what happens if I drink a few drops of it? After about half as much strengthening as the original, there is no need to die instantly. That’s the best result if I ask that many questions. However, there was a trap hidden there. By staying half-heartedly enhanced, the burden on the un-enhanced parts increases by tens of times, and as a result, they reduce their life expectancy. And at the most, they lose their lives within ten days. The reason why the dragonkin who fought Isario lost their lives must be the result of ignoring the burden and continuing to give it their all.Isario’s voice seemed to have more killing intent than ever before.
