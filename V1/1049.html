But as we grew up in Gordicia, we’ll get outta here!

However, something strange soon appeared.

To my surprise, they started to set up more wards than just the water dragonkin. Rather than constantly covering the entire area, they seemed to be set up so that they could react to our attack and set up thick wards only in that area.

Moreover, even if the attack was successful, a similar phenomenon would occur and the damage would be transferred to a smaller ship.

They must have been taking various measures to protect the major ships. They were clearly designed to be powerful enough to destroy the fleet on their own.

So, this means that they are prepared for this much defense?

Apparently, he wasn’t flying, but created ice sheets in the air and used them as a foothold to jump. He seemed to be using wind magic to help him jump.

He approached Fran while fluttering his black cloak, which looked like a trench coat. Was it the horns that grew out of his temples? I wondered if he was a demonkin, but I felt something was odd with his growth...

『Looks like he’s robbed of his will』

It was the slave collar. Moreover, it seemed that they were somehow deprived of their will and turned into a puppet.
