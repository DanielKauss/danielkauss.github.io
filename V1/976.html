This was our greatest chance now that everyone was struggling to get up. Everyone knew that.

It would be impossible to swallow this huge body. However, the shadow on the move was like a bottomless swamp, making it harder to stand on the ground than on the average person.

Blade Rondo, the level 8 Holy Sword Arts, was basically a series of ultra-high-speed slashes. About twelve slashes were unleashed in three seconds, causing major wounds on the giant-shaped knees.

It was originally a technique to attack multiple enemies, but Fran concentrated her attack on a single point. The current Fran was able to use even a higher-level Holy Sword Art quite well.

But that doesn’t mean the giant-type has just been defeated.

I guess they were trying to minimize the damage as much as possible.
