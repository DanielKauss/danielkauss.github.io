Ron. It was a quiet sound. But it was filled with a huge amount of Divine Power. For some reason, it made me tremble. Was I impressed by just one sound? Or was it fear? The saintess stood beside us in shock, surrounded by a large number of instruments. She put her fingers on the small harp in her hand and lightly clawed it. As if to respond to it, the instruments began to play a sound. They started with the key, then followed by wooden tubes, brass tubes, and percussion instruments. The number of sounds increased one after another. The song, which was slow at first, gradually increased in speed and turned into a song so intense that it looked like hard rock or metal. But it didn’t end there. The ever-increasing uptempo song turned into a song that made the listener feel uneasy, and finally, it was transformed into a song that could not be played so fast. The magic power released without a pause turned into a wave that enveloped the battlefield and shone brightly. However, the sound suddenly cut off. I wondered what was going on, but it seemed to be something like a prelude to what used to be.

「Don’t let your wind get away. Get out of your way, don’t lose.」

「Either way, an end was approaching.」

Sophie’s words were sinister, even though they were mysterious. The combination of the high-pitched hymn of an instrument and the high-pitched voice of Sophie’s song was like an opera, created a music that would make people’s minds happy, or rather, make them feel somewhat uneasy.

「Everyone lament, sorrow, fear, and destruction.」

The song of an adventurer that I had heard before was a song of energy that could be used to move forward toward the future. But like the lyrics, it must be a song of destruction. There was a sound of hope and a sound of despair in the music.

《Instability of the individual named giant antidemons detected. The internal magic has decreased.》

The effect seemed to be completely different. If the adventurer’s song was the top buff to strengthen one’s comrades, then the song of destruction must have some kind of debuffing effect that weakened enemy power. Sophie’s voice was transformed into a blueish-black light and struck by the giant antidemons over and over again. I could feel the magic power of the giant antidemons diminishing with each impact. Moreover, was the surface of their bodies being gradually being whittled down? The antidemons’ bodies were beginning to crumble as if they were starting to fade.

「Come now, destroy me! I will do as you specified!」

Sophie’s song had an effect on them, and the presence of the giant antidemons was clearly less than before. However, there were also those who were just as weak as Sophie.

「My madness is like a curse!」

Blood tears flowed from both of Sophie’s eyes as she exclaimed. Her face was still pale, and her lips were covered in blood. The recoil from the song of destruction must have been enough to dull Sophie’s vitality, but Sophie did not stop singing. But Sophie did not stop singing. She continued her oratory performance with a shout from the King’s position. Her claws had snapped and peeled, and her blood was pouring into the harp’s strings in a bright red. Finally, the song of destruction ended.

「Just as the end was approaching, annihilating me fast!」

The recoil from the song of destruction must have been chipping away at Sophie’s vitality. But Sophie did not stop singing. She continued her oratorium performance with a high voice as if she was in her last breath. Her claws had been broken and peeled, and her blood was pouring down on the harp’s strings in a bright red. Finally, the song of destruction ended.

「Just as the end was approaching, annihilating me fast!」

Just as she coughed up her last words, Sophie fell to the ground, bleeding from her mouth and eyes.
