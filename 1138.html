She continued to block the super fireballs released from the fort for a dozen seconds.

After the heat release had stopped, the situation around us had changed completely.

Smoke and sunlight still rose from the burned plains, and countless blacks that used to be human were lying on top of it. The heat didn’t seem to be enough to make ashes, but the devastation was even more conspicuous when the human form was still alive.

This was exactly what a hell was like.

The adventurers were stunned, completely silent in the face of the devastation.

The army of nobles that had escaped before was annihilated. Everything from top to bottom was burned evenly, without exception.

Nearly 5,000 people lost their lives in a single attack.

On the other hand, about half of the nobles and knights who had started to retreat with the adventurers were still alive. Well, many of them were half-dead from the heatwave. Even if the flames did not directly reach them, their entire bodies were burned from the heat, and their throats were swollen and covered.

It seems that they weren’t just protecting adventurers, their skills were only effective against adventurers.

Why? No skill would only work on people registered with the Adventurer’s Guild, right?
