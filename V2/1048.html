『Let’s attack the water dragonkin with everything we’ve got!』

Now that we hadn’t been found yet, we could unleash the best blow with our power accumulated to the limit. That would sink the water dragonkin and reduce the number of confused enemies. It seemed that their defenses were even greater than those of the old type. However, they were not expected to be able to prevent Urushi’s first blow. In fact, the purpose was to scatter the defenses by purposely blocking a wide area attack. Only one of them, a water dragon ship or a water dragon, would be able to target it. Compared to that, thunder magic had a much wider range and could even influence the other ships through the sea. When there were many enemies, it meant that Inazom was more convenient than Mezzolma. A bunch of white lightning released from the sky was intertwined in the air and rained on the water dragon ship. It was not only fired at the same time, but it was calculated to be more powerful. It was a possible feat thanks to the information processing skills I had acquired in Gordicia and the Great Wizard. We managed to get more than 10,000 magic power from this one-shot, but it’s okay. It was worth that much.
