Who is the commander of this swarm? Or is they all? Or does they have something stronger? But these guys are clearly special.

Twisted Horn was the strongest antidemon we had fought so far. I didn’t think it was that strong, but it was much stronger than the red dragon-type antidemons, which were stronger than the red dragon-type ones.

Their level of enhancement was comparable to Fran’s. Velmeria, with her light blue scales all over her body, was in a Divine Dragon Form, probably regaining the power she had when she went on a rampage in the Royal Capital.

My appraisal told me that he would become a Holy Beast. Maybe this was the final destination of all the Beastmen other than the Ten Primordial Beastmen.

I guess that means he’s honing his tricky movements and offensive abilities.

There seemed to be almost no auto-destruction. This would prolong our self-destruction.

Her stats, except for her agility, have not increased much.

However, she had an Innate Skill called “Twisty”. It seemed to be a forced debuffing ability that greatly weakened the opponent she touched.

Mea was probably the one who made the most terrifying evolution. She was in her White Beast state, but her stats had exceeded 1,000 across the board, and “Divine Flame” had been added to her list of skills.

The white flames all over her body were emitting Divine Attributes, and the auto counter was not enough to be described as evil.

With just a single touch of it, the white divine flames spread over her entire body. And she never disappeared until she was gone.
