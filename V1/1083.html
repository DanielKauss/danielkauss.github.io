Chapter 1083

The dusty wilderness. The trees and even the undergrowth were not growing well, and the dry and reddish ground continued.

This rugged land lying between the Kranzel Kingdom and the Shallus Kingdom was not worth using, so both countries did not insist on their territories and split the center as their border.

In the middle of the wilderness, a horrible army advanced with silence.

All it could hear was a strangely hard sound created by the march and an unpleasant sound emitted from the scraping of armor. I couldn’t hear the sound of breathing, let alone the words being spoken.

And of course, that was all that mattered.

It wasn’t just people who made the march.

The most common ones were probably the undead. They were zombie soldiers who wore armor and continued to walk at the same speed with their spears on their backs.

The undead body and equipment were of poor quality, showing inconspicuousness as a force to be reckoned with. However, since there were so many of them, they might be of use for occupiers and diversion. There were probably well over 5,000 of them.

Leading the undead in this army were golems as glistening as precious stones.

It was not a gem, but a golem made of a magic stone. The sight of more than a hundred deformed golems marching in a row was even spectacular.

We were quite late for this out-of-people army, and the troops of what seemed to be the Kingdom of Shallus’ army were following us. Were they afraid of the undead or were planning to use them to dispel them? Maybe they were about a kilometer away?
