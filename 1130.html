Chapter 1130

「I can see it! It’s all around me!」

『Just as one of Avenger’s men said』

「Nn.」

Based on the information we obtained, we headed for a fort in the Raidos Kingdom, but the end of the battle had already opened.

Moreover, with three sides surrounded by enemies, the Kranzel army is in a very dangerous situation.

Just like the in the report, the red knights were there too. Forrund was facing him, but was he an opponent that even that man could not defeat?

How can we save the Kranzel army? It’s not something we can just rush in without thinking.

As I was wondering about what to do while running to the battlefield, something strange happened.

The white light enveloped the adventurers. Some of the knights were also glowing. I could feel the healing mana, so it didn’t seem like an attack from the enemy. The center of their mana was around the adventurers.

An enormous amount of magic power gushed out from a demonkin, even a hint of kindness. It was thick, long horns, and a huge body on the level of Ashwrath. Even though it was seen from a distance with viewing skills, it was intimidating and powerful.

No one in Alessa was that conspicuous of a demonkin, right? Was she a valiant warrior from another guild?

No, wait a minute. That face... Could it be you’re Donad? Donadrond?

He’s changed too much in such a short time! His body has swelled up, his horns have grown, and the quality of the magic power he releases has been considerably altered.

But now that the entire battlefield was perplexed, this was our chance.

『Fran, let’s go rescue Forrund first.』

(Got it.)

The young magician facing Forrund was probably the Crimson Flame Knight from the intel. I heard that he was strong, but if we and Forrund fought at the same time, he would not be an opponent we could beat.

Hearing her abilities, I guess she was just like Isario’s subordinates.

And we had more forces than just the antidemon brigade. If we could send them to destroy the enemy undead, we could save the adventurers too.

In other words, we should be able to make it in time.

「Hey, he’s not going to die」

「Huh! What is it, my priestess!?」

What responded to Fran’s words with great force was the undead running behind her. A vicious ghoul that emitted evil energy. Yes, this was the Genoside Ghoul commander who had attacked me. The Genoside Ghouls under his command were also following closely.

The ghouls, who called themselves Avengers of the Black Skeleton Army, began to follow Fran for a certain reason.

A few hours ago.

My Evil Spirit Conquest skill had succeeded in abusing the Genoside Ghoul.

Don’t move, obey. Stop the fight. With that in mind, I manipulated the evil inside the ghouls, and it certainly slowed down their movements.

『This is the perfect opportunity for us to kill your Evil Spirits! Slayer, slayer, and slayer!』

「Nn! Haaa!」

「Shiiiit!」

In order to defeat the ghoul, Fran slashed at it with the Evil Crusher at full force. However, the blade did not slash at the ghoul.

「O priestess! My priestess! Did I wrong! Permit me!」

「?」

『Huh?』

The ghouls suddenly dissipated hostility and got down on their knees. The killing intent that had been pointed at them up until now was also lurking around.

「......Are you sure?」

「Haa! I have a revelation from the Goddess in my divine vessel that I will obey you!」

The other ghouls also gathered around Fran and knelt, matching her commander. All of them except a few that had already been defeated.

「Nn. But I can’t borrow the power of the Evil God’s piece」

As I was thinking about this, the piece of the Evil God inside me wriggled. It wasn’t that it had unleashed tremendous power. However, a dense piece of Evil Spirit overflowed slightly, blessing the ghouls.

「Oooh! Ooooh! O my God! I understand!」

「「「「Yeah!」」」」

The ghouls let loose a roar of joy. If the tear glands had been alive, they would have definitely broken through.

The ghouls, rubbing their knees and foreheads against the ground, lamented Fran.

「O priestess! Our priestess! You truly are a priestess!」

「Must have been the priestess!」

「Guide us!」

Was it my imagination that the pieces of the Evil Gods were giving off a rather proud atmosphere? Anyway, the Evil Conquest I used and the pieces of the Evil Gods I used were both sure to impress and knock the ghouls out.

The sudden craziness of the ghouls, or rather, their surrender, bewildered both the valiant soldiers and Mareficent. Even this Persona could feel their bewilderment.

Well, you’re right. Fran was suddenly starting to be revered. Moreover, it seemed that Mareficent sensed the evil spirits because the Evil God Fragment had taken its own actions.

「Black Lightning Princess-dono? What the hell is this......?」

「...Somehow, this has happened」

「Seliadot! Are you even listening to me!?」

「It’s okay. They had the power to manipulate evil energy, so they were no longer enemies.」

「Well, Black Lightning Princess-dono is fine. So, for now, I guess we have no choice but to accept it」

She must have remembered the unwritten rule of not forcing adventurers to listen to each other. She didn’t ask any further.

The warriors looked at Fran with an adored look as if she had easily controlled the highest level undead.

「Y-Yeah! Captain! Curry!」

「Morning priestess! You’re a cat ear priestess!」

「Even the Cat Ear priestess! That’s impressive! Captain of the Cat Ear priestess! Curry!」

「Must have been the priestess! With those black cat ears, hear the word of the Gods!」

Somehow, all of them except for Mareficent and Persona were in a weird situation where they all worshiped Fran.

『Let’s talk to the ghouls for now』

「Nn.」

Hopefully we can get some info on the Kingdom of Raidos though.
