Sophie and Urushi appeared alongside a group of people.

Her song――or rather, their song covered the entire battlefield. It was an adventurer’s song sung by thousands of people.

Space? The air? Anyway, the entire battlefield slowly healed Fran and Mea’s wounds, emitting a faint golden light as if in response to the song.

I ignored the effects of the divine attribute as well, and me, Fran, and Mea were beginning to recover. I guess it was due to this magical power that possessed the divine attribute.

It seemed that the backlash of using the divine attribute was being healed by the divine attribute.

The antidemons, on the other hand, stopped moving in confusion. With their entire body enveloped in powerful magic power, they seemed to have no idea what to do next.

It must be the same as when the artificial intelligence was bugging in an unexpected situation and stopping its movement. That was happening all over the battlefield. The range of effects was terrifying.

Many people were standing behind Sophie and Charon as they smiled. What they were wearing was normal clothes. Some of them were wearing armor on top of it, but most of them were just wearing pots.

They had a wide variety of weapons, including rusted spears and swords, as well as pans and horned materials. Moreover, they were not wearing them at all.

They were ordinary citizens by any stretch of the imagination.

But he had a brave expression on his face as he lined up behind Sophie. I couldn’t help but feel a strange sense of confidence just by looking at him.

There were so many people willing to stand on the battlefield with us. That fact was important.

The ability to control instruments? However, all the instruments were emitting tremendous magic power. combined with all of them, they would far exceed me.

It wasn’t an ordinary magic tool. Could it be the Earth Sword Gaia that released the closest magic power I’ve ever seen? However, there was no intimidating atmosphere to it.

Rather, it had a thousand of them, and how would they attack me? How would they be treated as a dull vessel? Also, its magic conductivity would be a magnitude lower than that.
