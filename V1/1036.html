So many fierce battles, so many strong opponents. I wondered if only one of them ever got up, but after fighting in large numbers, Fran’s level rose to over 70%.

In fact, an ordinary low-level, medium-level antidemon would have tens of thousands――no, maybe even hundreds of thousands of experience value.

In fact, when we heard about the adventurers around us, we found that many of them had leveled up more than 10.

It would take a terrifying amount of experience for Fran to level up in the future. Besides, the quality of her enemies might be important too.

With a mere goblin, I didn’t know how much I had to defeat. There was even a possibility that I wouldn’t get any experience.

In the future, it might be more important for her to hone her skills than her level. In fact, it was not only the level that Fran gained this time.

Not to mention the blessings of the Beast and Insect Gods. I have acquired several skills that are difficult to obtain in a short period of time, such as fear resistance, patience, sleep resistance, regeneration, and perseverance, that require me to put myself in an extraordinary situation.

Of course, the battle on this continent was not the only reason. The various fierce battles that had occurred since Fran met me had given her these skills.

Also, skills such as distance learning, kicking skills, morale boost, and equilibria were skills that I have lived up to. My blinking skills had reached the max level, and now I have developed a higher-level skill called ‘Sudden Movement’. My agility has been sharpened.

The increase in stats was probably not as huge as it was when she increased her stats from 69 to 70%. I guess that was the result of the combination of the effects of the Divine Beast Transformation and the bonus at a good level of kiri.

Still, HP and MP stats increased by more than 100, and agility increased by about 70%. Thanks to that, our stats exceeded 1,000.

All of our other stats had grown by about 30~40.

Like I thought, after all, the increase in status from the level of ‘70 increased without a hitch, and she seems to be able to become stronger rapidly. It would be quite difficult to raise the level though.

She also received a title, “Substantial Beasts of the Divine Beast”.

It is said to be a title given to those who were able to sense the blood of a Divine Beast flowing through their body. Was it manifested by manipulating the Divine Attribute with the help of the Beast and Insect God’s blessings?

The effect was rather vague, as it strengthened her instinct as a Divine Beast. I didn’t quite understand what it was, but it might be a trigger for her to become a Divine Beast.

「At any rate, we need to take her to a place where she can rest. What do you think?」

That means that the various people we’ve seen in the past may not actually have some of the most important skills or titles. Can they be seen now?

Urushi was also at the level of 61, and his stats had increased to more than Fran. I guess this was probably because he was a magic beast. His HP had increased by 250 now.

Compared to her stats, her skills weren’t much improved. Unlike normal humans, she probably didn’t learn many skills.

If we lose to a magic beast in terms of skills and status, humans will perish in no time at all.

However, she still had the skills for hauling and fortitude. Maybe it was because she transported so many people? The reason for her indomitableness was probably because she had experienced a tough battle like Fran’s.

『But I guess both of you are at level 61』

It was thanks to Fran’s efforts that I was able to reach this level in such a short time. I would admit that my power was great. But no matter how strong I was, it was Fran who wielded it.

The current result was only possible because of Fran’s hard work.

Even though he was physically lined up against Beast King Rigdis (probably), that was only a matter of numbers.

The only problem was that he was inferior in terms of skills and had completely lost in terms of experience.
