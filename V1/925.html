No matter how high their regenerative power was, they would soon be unable to keep up with the black lightning and slashes imbued with the divine attribute.

As if her actions had signaled, the three horns had collapsed, their slender bodies scattered all over the earth.

But we hadn’t defeated it yet.

Now I could see the wreckage of the antidemons was desperately trying to move. If left unchecked, it would regenerate and start to move sooner or later.

I’d expect as much from a top-level special individual, the Twisted Horn of Castel. In the face of such an antidemon, Fran thrust her right palm at his opponent.

As if in response to Fran’s concentration, the magic power cloaking her was transformed into black lightning and raged through the area. Then, a large amount of black lightning that gushed out gathered in front of Fran’s palm and shrunk.

That was a tremendous power. Was it the Black Tiger Lightning Rush? I guess that was the secret technique of the Divine Beast, the Black Heavenly Tiger.
