Chapter 1124

We’ve been training at Fran’s Bootcamp for the past three days.

The valiant soldiers had been surprisingly transformed into serious people. They had crisp movements, worked well together with their friends, and at the end of the day, they had curry and talked about the wonders of work.

She had already returned the carriage, and her escort was in a perfect state. Originally, there was no problem with her abilities. Once her personality had improved, there was no need to worry.

That doesn’t sound like a bit of a brainwashing technique, but it’s better than being captured by a magic beast.

Actually, at the end of the second day, Mareficent had revealed the truth to us.

The only thing that started to happen was that Fran had noticed the presence of the spirit.

At night, Fran suddenly looked around. There was no sign of any magic beast around, and her eyes were neither on the valiant soldiers nor the Marquis Saints.

For some reason, I felt like I was wandering around in an empty sky.

『What’s wrong?』

(It’s the spirit)

『What?』

(There’s many of them.)

Several spirits seemed to be floating around. I couldn’t feel any sign of them, but I thought of a way to find them.

I activated Spirit’s Hand and tried to spread it around me along with my telekinesis. As I felt it, something was slightly touching me.

They escaped in surprise the moment they touched it, but they couldn’t see it and couldn’t feel it, so they must be a spirit.

Maybe there are at least five or six of them?

(I know this presence.)

『What?』

(Klimt’s Spirit of Water and the Spirit of Wind)

It seems that before she knew it, Fran was able to sense the difference between spirits.

By Klimut’s spirit, I mean that he was secretly guarding us? Or was he watching over us?
