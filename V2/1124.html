Chapter 1124

Three days have passed since Fran’s Bootcamp continued, and the valiant warriors had been transformed into amazingly serious people. They moved with a tingle, worked together with their friends, and at the end of the day, they ate the curry and talked about the wonders of their work. They had already returned the carriage, but their escort was in a perfect state. Originally, there were no problems with their abilities. As their personality had improved, I didn’t feel uneasy. It may not sound like they were a little brainwashed, but it was better than being captured by Mareficent. It may not sound like she was a little brainwashed, but it’s better than being captured by Mareficent. Actually, at the end of the second day, Mareficent had revealed the truth to us. The beginning of the incident was that Fran had noticed the presence of a spirit. At night, Fran suddenly looked around to see the surroundings. There was no sign of any magic beast, and her eyes were not on the soldiers or Mareficents. For some reason, she was wandering around an empty sky.

『What happened?』

(The spirits are there)

『What?』

(There’s a lot of them)

It seemed that several spirits were floating around. I couldn’t feel any sign of them, but I thought of a way to find them. I activated my Spirit Hand and tried to expand it around me along with my telekinesis. As I did so, I felt a slight touch of something. The moment I touched it, I ran away with a surprised look, but I was sure that there was a spirit that I couldn’t see and couldn’t feel. I wonder if there were at least five or six of them.

(I know this presence)

『What?』

(Klimut’s water spirit and the wind spirit)

Before she knew it, Fran was able to sense the difference between the spirits. When she said that it was Klimut’s spirit, does that mean that she was secretly escorted by her? Or is he a guard?
