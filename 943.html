Chapter 943

After releasing the power of his Blade of Origin, Alpha, he plunged into the antidemon army by himself.

100,000 vs. one person. But we didn’t have any worries as we were watching it.

The holy knights didn’t even go to support them, really fighting alone.

Maybe the holy knights were being expelled along the way. Also, maybe they were trying to wipe out any attempted attackers?

Besides, they’d be completely involved if we were near that Addler. It would be difficult to cover for them.

Each time that the white magic-powered Addle wielded its Divine Sword, a tsunami-like shockwave was released, swallowing the antidemons.

Not to mention the power, it was just a wide range. Fran could fire a flying slash, but it was impossible to spread it that far.

There was no expression on Addle’s face as she slayed the antidemons. I also felt uncomfortable with her refreshing face, which seemed to be the work of before the Divine Sword Release, but I felt something strange when she suddenly lost her expression.

He didn’t seem to have any intention of missing any of the antidemons, and was politely crushing them from the lower class antidemons without being able to target the commander at once.

There was no disturbance.

The wielder of the divine sword wielded solid power to make his enemies disappear from the inside out. Such a scene continued until the very end.

「It’s over」

『Yeah... It really was the same until the very end.』

The commander, who had been cut in half and defeated just a few seconds ago, was not weak. The current Fran, who had no Divine Beast Form, must have had a hard time.

I’m not saying that we will lose, but it will take a good amount of time even if we try our best.

They were instantly wiped out, just like lower class antidemons.

As I’d thought, the power of the Divine Sword was ridiculous. And it seemed that Addle was raised to demonstrate its full power.

With that in mind, it would not be an exaggeration to say that she was the strongest person in the world when she used the Divine Sword Release.

She was a beautiful girl with reddish-purple hair tied into twin tails, who looked a little older than Fran. Moreover, she didn’t look like an adventurer.

She was wearing a gothic dress based on black. Not a dress armor, but a miniskirt dress. She might be using a monster material, but she didn’t look like a defensive one.

For such a conspicuous person, she would remember it at the time of the meeting.

Her face was still expressionless, but no sense of hostility. Who was she? It was obvious that Fran had some business to attend to.

Commander Hagane? Could it be that she was in a carriage protected by the old soldiers? If so, this girl named Hydrangea was――.

That’s right. It wouldn’t be possible for many people to ride that carriage, and if there were other people besides the commander, it was most likely the wielder of the Divine Sword.

If used, it would definitely go out of control and eventually kill Berserk, the worst Divine Sword in the world.

The wielder is, to put it bluntly, a sacrifice. Is this girl their sacrifice?
