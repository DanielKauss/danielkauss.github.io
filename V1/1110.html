Klimut stared at the spirit, releasing magic power and standing on his knees.

The Great Spirit of Wind, on the other hand, did not move much either.

The reason they were not attacked now was that they were near Klimt. The Great Spirit could not attack them because it might implicate Klimt, who recognized them as a magician.

It must have been thanks to that fact that Urushi and Zenos were safe. That was also the reason why they suddenly stopped attacking us after we passed through the wall of tornadoes.

However, if she attacked, she would be recognized as a complete enemy and would be subject to a fierce counterattack. Even if she knew this, Klimt seemed to have been cornered so much that she had to ask Fran, a child, to attack her.
